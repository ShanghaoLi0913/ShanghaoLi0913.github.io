About

I am a computer science Ph.D. student at the University of Illinois Chicago (UIC), fortunate to be advised by Prof. Philip S. Yu in his Big Data and Social Computing (BDSC) Lab.

Generally, I am interested in building trustworthy knowledge-augmented language models.

I have worked on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (GraphRAG) , where I developed methods for hallucination detection and interpretable evaluation metrics.
